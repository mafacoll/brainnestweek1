Carrot cake

I firmly believe that you should cake once in a while everyday.
However you should also eat you vegetables, and this next cake allows you to do both, at the same time!

INGRIDIENTS:
  • Brown Sugar
  • Oil
  • Applesauce
  • Freshly Shredded Carrots
  • Spreadable cheesecake
INSTURCTIONS:
  1. Line the cake pans with parchment
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients
  3. Whisk the liquid and add to the dry mixture
  4. Bake the cake
  5. Assemble and frost once the cake is cooled
