Carrot cake

I firmly believe that you should cakeonce in a while everyday.
However you should also eat you vegetables, and this next cake allows you to do both, at the same time!

INGRIDIENTS:
  • Cake Flour
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • Butter and oil
  • Buttermilk
INSTURCTIONS:
  1. Preheat the oven and grease two pans
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients
  3. Bake the cake
  4. Beat the butter with sugar to make the frosting
  5. Assemble and frost once the cake is cooled
  6. Refrigerate the cake for around 60 min before eating
