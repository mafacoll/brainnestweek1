Red Velvet Cake

We start with red velvet cake because it's a personal favorite of mine.
Now, to be honest, I don't know why since it's basically vanilla cake tinted red, but if it's on the menu I'm ordering it.
How to make me very happy and make this cake at home:

INGRIDIENTS:
  • Cake Flour
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • Butter and oil
  • Buttermilk
INSTURCTIONS:
  1. Preheat the oven and grease two pans
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients
  3. Bake the cake
  4. Beat the butter with sugar to make the frosting
  5. Assemble and frost once the cake is cooled
  6. Refrigerate the cake for around 60 min before eating

And of course... ENJOY ENJOY ENJOY but leave me a piece
