Lemon cake

When life gives you lemons it's truly a pitty to make lemonade when you have the option to make this fantstic cake.
Being someone who loves citric flavours, lemon has a very soft spot in my heart.
If recently you're getting too many lemons I recommend you bake this cake, and that you share it with me of course.

INGRIDIENTS:
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Lemon Zest and lemon juice
  • Butter and Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
INSTURCTIONS:
  1. Preheat the oven and grease the pan
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients
  3. Bake the cake
  4. Combine the icing ingridients
  5. Once cold, use the mixture to assamble the cake

Enjoy the cake and be positive, life won't always give you the yellow fruit.
